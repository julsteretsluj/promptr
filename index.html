Promptr

Promptr is a prompting checklist web app that helps people with executive functioning challenges complete everyday routines. It breaks tasks such as showering, dishes, getting dressed, homework, and shopping into clear one-step-at-a-time prompts.

With an account, Promptr lets you save lasting custom checklists, build a daily plan, and receive optional reminders. You can sign in with email or Google Sign-In to sync your data.
