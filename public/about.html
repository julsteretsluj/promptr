About Promptr

Purpose of the application

Promptr is a web application that helps people complete everyday tasks by breaking them into clear, one-step-at-a-time prompts. It is designed especially for people with executive functioning challenges (for example ADHD-related task initiation difficulties).

Promptr provides:

  • Preset checklists for routines such as showering, dishes, cleaning, homework, and shopping
  • Custom checklists you can create and reuse
  • Daily planning so you can schedule which checklists to do
  • Optional reminders by email and Google Calendar

Google Sign-In

Promptr uses Google Sign-In so you can create an account and sync your checklists and plans. If you connect Google Calendar, Promptr can create reminder events you choose to schedule.
