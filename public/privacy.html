Privacy Policy

Last updated: 23 July 2026

Promptr (“we”, “us”, or “the app”) helps people break everyday tasks into clear next steps. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices. By using Promptr, you agree to this policy.

1. Who we are

Promptr is operated by Jules Kitto. Contact: juleskittoastrop@gmail.com.

2. Information we collect

Depending on how you use Promptr, we may collect:

  • Account information — email address, display name, and authentication details when you sign up with email/password or Google Sign-In.
  • Profile & preferences — timezone, text size, accent color, motion preferences, and home layout settings.
  • App content you create — custom checklists, daily plans, scheduled times, completion status, and related notes.
  • Reminder settings — whether email and/or Google Calendar reminders are enabled, and how far in advance reminders should fire.
  • Google account data (optional) — if you connect Google, we may receive your Google account email and a token that lets us create calendar events on your behalf when you enable Calendar reminders. We only request calendar access needed to create reminder events you schedule in Promptr.
  • Technical data — basic logs needed to operate authentication and deliver reminders (for example, reminder send status).

If you use Promptr as a guest without signing in, custom checklists may be stored only in your browser on that device.

3. How we use information

  • Provide, maintain, and improve Promptr
  • Authenticate you and sync your checklists and plans across devices
  • Send reminder emails you opt into (sent from our Gmail account on behalf of Promptr)
  • Create Google Calendar events you opt into
  • Respond to support requests and protect the security of the service

We do not sell your personal information.

4. Where data is stored

Account and app data are stored using Supabase (authentication and database). Reminder emails may be sent using Google’s Gmail API from our operator Gmail account. Calendar events are created using Google Calendar API when you connect your Google account.

5. Google user data

Promptr’s use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

We use Google user data only to provide or improve user-facing features in Promptr (sign-in and optional calendar reminders). We do not use Google user data for serving advertisements, and we do not transfer Google user data to third parties except as needed to operate those features or as required by law.

6. Sharing

We may share information only with:

  • Service providers that host or operate core infrastructure (for example, Supabase and Google)
  • Authorities when required by law

7. Data retention

We keep your account data while your account is active. You may delete custom checklists and plan items in the app. To request account deletion or a full data erase, email juleskittoastrop@gmail.com and we will process the request within a reasonable time.

8. Your choices

  • You can use guest mode without creating an account
  • You can disable email or calendar reminders in Reminder settings
  • You can disconnect Google access via your Google Account permissions
  • You can request deletion of your account data by contacting us

9. Children’s privacy

Promptr is not directed at children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, contact us and we will take appropriate steps to delete it.

10. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will change when we do. Continued use of Promptr after changes means you accept the updated policy.

11. Contact

Questions about privacy: juleskittoastrop@gmail.com
